WHO WE ARE

We started over 35 years ago with a no-kill sanctuary in Utah.
Today. we're helping homeless pets in every corner of the
country. >>

WORKING
TOGETHER

All across America, pets in shelter are getting connected with
loving new families, increasing the national save rate from 64%
in 2015 to 83% in 2020.>>

OUR IMPACT

Last year, shelters partnering with Best Friends reduced the
number of pets killed at 3.5 times the rate of other shelters.
That's 63,374 cats and dogs saved.>>
